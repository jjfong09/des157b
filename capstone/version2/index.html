Congrats!

By completing this quiz, you've explored how different UI design trends affect the way we perceive products, specifically emphasizing expressive versus minimalistic design. Minimalism is often associated with clarity and functionality, while expressiveness leans into personality and emotion. As trends constantly cycle, we're seeing styles that merge, evolve, and reappear in new forms.

While minimalistic design has been dominant these past few years, espcially in tech, there's a growing shift toward more expressive interfaces. As technology becomes more integrated into daily life, design may play a bigger role in shaping not just usability, but emotional resonance.
