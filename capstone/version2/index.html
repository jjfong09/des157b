Visual Design Trends Quiz

An experiment to test how we perceive our digital products

But first some background...

The change in design trends over the years not only reflect shifts in aesthetics, but also shifts culture, and technology. Understanding them helps us recognize why certain styles feel "modern" or "dated," and how visual choices shape our experience with the product we use everyday. Below are some popular design trends from he past few decades, largely influenced by new tech and major companies from Apple to Airbnb.

This interactive quiz is designed to test how we see, interpret, and predict interface design.

From skeuomorphic buttons to AI-generated dashboards, the way interfaces look and feel is shaped by the tools we use, the tech we adopt, and the cultural trends we absorb, and they often influence us without us even noticing.
